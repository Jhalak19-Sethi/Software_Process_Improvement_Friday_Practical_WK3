# Core Game Overview

## Game Type
- Survival
- Board Game
- 1 vs Many Players (Fixed: 4 players total)

---

# Initial Game Start
- Each player begins with **25 Victory Points**
- All players start **scattered** across different starting tiles on the board
- A shared **Question Deck** is placed at the center

---

# Win Condition
- First player to reach **50 Victory Points** wins the game

---

# Dice System
- Players roll **2 × 6‑sided dice**
- **Roll Total > 6** → Player is allowed to take an action this turn

---

# Board Action Tiles

## 🟥 Bad Tiles
- **Penalty to Next Roll**  
  - Suffer a negative modifier on your next dice roll
- **Ambush**  
  - Lose **2 Victory Points**
- **Move Backwards**  
  - Move backwards a number of spaces specified by the tile

---

## 🟩 Good Tiles
- **Steal Points (Dice Roll)**  
  - Roll 1 die → Steal that many points from any player
- **Gain Points (Dice Roll)**  
  - Roll 1 die → Gain that many Victory Points
- **Left‑Side Steal**  
  - Steal **10 Victory Points** from the player to your left

---

## ⏱️ Mini‑Game Tile (Timer Challenge)
- Player draws a card from the **Question Deck**
- A timer is set based on the **card type**
- Player must answer the question clearly within the time limit
- Other players judge whether the answer is acceptable
- **Failure → Lose 10 Victory Points**
- **Success → Move forward 5 spaces and immediately take another turn**

---

## ⚔️ Player Collision Rule
**If two players land on the same tile:**
- Both players roll 1 die
- Higher roll wins
- Winner **steals 5 Victory Points** from the loser

---

# Game Workflow

1. All players start with **25 Victory Points**
2. All players begin scattered around the board
3. Players take turns rolling dice and performing actions based on:
   - Dice roll conditions  
   - Tile effects  
   - Player interactions  
4. First player to reach **50 Victory Points** wins

---

# Turn Structure

1. **Start of Turn Effects**  
   (Resolve penalties, bonuses, or ongoing effects)
2. **Roll Dice**  
   (2 × 6‑sided dice)
3. **Check Roll Condition**  
   - If total > 6 → Player may act  
   - If total ≤ 6 → Turn ends
4. **Move**  
   - Move the number of spaces indicated by the dice
5. **Optional Actions**  
   - Based on the tile landed on
6. **End of Turn**

---

# Board Structure

1. The board is a **looping track**
2. Only the tile a player **lands on** is activated
3. Tile Types:
   - **Bad Tiles**
   - **Good Tiles**
   - **Mini‑Game Tiles**
   - **Empty Tiles** (no effect)

---

# Question Deck Structure

## Card Types
- **Trivia Cards**  
  - General knowledge, simple facts  
  - Short timer (e.g., 10–15 seconds)

- **Logic Cards**  
  - Riddles, patterns, reasoning  
  - Medium timer (e.g., 15–20 seconds)

- (Optional future expansion: Survival Cards, Challenge Cards)

## Card Outcome
- **Success:** Move forward 5 spaces and take another turn  
- **Failure:** Lose 10 Victory Points
